A static map that chronicles the United States Women's National Team on their journey to winning the 2015 FIFA Women's World Cup in Canada. The main map focuses on the cross-country travel that was required during the tournament by the USWNT, as well as those stadiums that were utilized and the attendance during those games. The illustrated infographics underneath the main text are meant to pull out meaningful statistics from the USWNT during the tournament as a whole, highlighting impressive feats. The game-by-game infographics along the bottom summarize each match by score, as well as other soccer-specific metrics like fouls and possession.

Tools: ArcMap, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator
